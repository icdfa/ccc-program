# Practice Exam 1

## Instructions

This practice exam consists of 50 multiple-choice questions and is designed to simulate the final certification exam. You should allow approximately 2 hours to complete the exam. The answer key is provided at the end.

---

### Questions

1.  **Which of the following best describes the primary role of a cybersecurity consultant?**
    a) To sell security products
    b) To provide expert advice and guidance on cybersecurity matters
    c) To manage the day-to-day operations of the security team
    d) To conduct penetration testing only

2.  **What is the primary purpose of a risk appetite statement?**
    a) To eliminate all risks
    b) To define the amount and type of risk that an organization is willing to accept
    c) To transfer all risks to a third party
    d) To document all known vulnerabilities

3.  **Which of the following is a key component of the "Protect" function in the NIST Cybersecurity Framework?**
    a) Identifying assets and vulnerabilities
    b) Implementing access control and data security measures
    c) Detecting security incidents
    d) Responding to and recovering from security incidents

4.  **What is the primary purpose of a Statement of Applicability (SoA) in ISO 27001?**
    a) To list all possible security controls
    b) To document which controls from Annex A are applicable and justified for inclusion or exclusion
    c) To serve as a contract with the certification body
    d) To define the scope of the ISMS

5.  **Under GDPR, what is the maximum fine for a serious infringement?**
    a) €10 million or 2% of global annual turnover, whichever is higher
    b) €20 million or 4% of global annual turnover, whichever is higher
    c) €50 million or 10% of global annual turnover, whichever is higher
    d) There is no maximum fine

## Program Development

The Certified Cybersecurity Consultant (CCC) program was developed by Aminu Idris to address the growing need for strategic cybersecurity consultants who can bridge the gap between technical security expertise and business advisory services.

### Vision

To transform experienced cybersecurity professionals into strategic consultants capable of advising organizations on complex security governance, risk, and compliance matters.

### Mission

To provide comprehensive, practical training that equips cybersecurity professionals with the knowledge, skills, and experience necessary to succeed as independent consultants or internal security strategists.

## Study Groups

We encourage you to form study groups with other students in the CCC program. You can use the community forums to find other students who are interested in forming a study group.

### Study Group Guidelines

- **Set a regular meeting time:** Meet at least once a week to discuss the course materials and assignments.
- **Assign roles:** Assign roles to each member of the group (e.g., facilitator, note-taker).
- **Come prepared:** Come to each meeting prepared to discuss the assigned readings and topics.
- **Be respectful:** Be respectful of other members of the group and their opinions.

## Course Description

The Certified Cybersecurity Consultant (CCC) program is a highly specialized, six-month training curriculum designed for experienced cybersecurity professionals seeking to transition into consulting or strategic security roles. This program bridges the gap between technical expertise and strategic business advisory, equipping graduates to function as independent consultants or internal security strategists. The Standard Track provides a balanced approach for professionals managing work commitments while pursuing certification.

---

## Program Learning Outcomes

Upon successful completion of this program, students will be able to:

1. **Strategic Consulting:** Demonstrate the ability to function as a trusted cybersecurity advisor, aligning security initiatives with business objectives and communicating effectively with executive leadership.

2. **Risk Management:** Conduct comprehensive qualitative and quantitative risk assessments, develop risk treatment strategies, and create multi-year security roadmaps that prioritize initiatives based on business impact.

3. **GRC Implementation:** Design, implement, and audit Information Security Management Systems (ISMS) based on ISO 27001, and ensure compliance with major regulatory frameworks including GDPR, HIPAA, PCI DSS, and CCPA.

4. **Security Architecture:** Design enterprise-level security architectures incorporating Zero Trust principles, cloud security strategies, and vendor risk management frameworks.

5. **Professional Excellence:** Deliver executive-level presentations, manage consulting engagements effectively, and produce professional deliverables that translate technical findings into business impact.

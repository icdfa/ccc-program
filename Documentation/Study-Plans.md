# Weekly Study Plans

## Introduction

This document provides three recommended study plans to help you structure your learning and complete the CCC program at a pace that suits your schedule. The program is designed to be approximately 320 hours of study, including reading, assignments, labs, and the capstone project.

---

## Study Plan Options

### 1. Intensive Track (4 Months)

**Commitment:** 20 hours per week

This track is for students who can dedicate a significant amount of time each week to the program.

- **Module 1:** 4 weeks (80 hours)
- **Module 2:** 4 weeks (80 hours)
- **Module 3:** 4 weeks (80 hours)
- **Module 4:** 4 weeks (80 hours)

### 2. Standard Track (6 Months)

**Commitment:** 13-14 hours per week

This track is for students who want to balance the program with other commitments.

- **Module 1:** 6 weeks (80 hours)
- **Module 2:** 6 weeks (80 hours)
- **Module 3:** 6 weeks (80 hours)
- **Module 4:** 6 weeks (80 hours)

### 3. Part-Time Track (8 Months)

**Commitment:** 10 hours per week

This track is for students who have limited time each week and want to complete the program at a more relaxed pace.

- **Module 1:** 8 weeks (80 hours)
- **Module 2:** 8 weeks (80 hours)
- **Module 3:** 8 weeks (80 hours)
- **Module 4:** 8 weeks (80 hours)

## Time Management Tips

- **Schedule Your Study Time:** Block out dedicated time in your calendar for studying.
- **Break It Down:** Break down each topic into smaller, manageable tasks.
- **Stay Consistent:** Consistency is key. Even a small amount of study each day is better than cramming.
- **Take Breaks:** Take regular breaks to avoid burnout.
- **Engage with the Community:** Participate in discussion forums to ask questions and learn from others.
- **Track Your Progress:** Use the checklists in each module to track your progress.

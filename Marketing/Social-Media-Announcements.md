## Press Release

**FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE**

**International Cybersecurity and Digital Forensics Academy Launches Certified Cybersecurity Consultant (CCC) Program**

**Open-Source Curriculum Available on GitHub; Applications Open January 4th, 2026**

**[City, State] – December 24, 2025** – The International Cybersecurity and Digital Forensics Academy (ICDFA) today announced the launch of its Certified Cybersecurity Consultant (CCC) Program, a comprehensive 320-hour training curriculum designed for experienced cybersecurity professionals seeking to transition into strategic consulting and advisory roles.

The CCC Program addresses a critical gap in the cybersecurity industry: the need for professionals who can bridge technical expertise with strategic business advisory. The program covers four core modules: Strategic Security Consulting & Business Alignment, Governance, Risk & Compliance (GRC) Deep Dive, Advanced Security Architecture & Solution Design, and Professional Consulting Skills.

"The cybersecurity industry needs professionals who can not only understand technical threats but also translate them into business impact and strategic recommendations," said Aminu Idris, AMCPN, Founder and Commandant of ICDFA and Program Director of the CCC Program. "The CCC Program is designed to develop these critical skills and prepare experienced professionals for high-impact consulting and leadership roles."

**Program Features:**

- **Flexible Learning Tracks:** Students can choose from Fast Track (4 months), Standard Track (6 months), or Part-Time Track (8 months) based on their schedule and learning preferences.

- **Industry Alignment:** The curriculum is aligned with the NICE Cybersecurity Workforce Framework and incorporates industry-leading frameworks including NIST CSF, ISO 27001, GDPR, HIPAA, and PCI DSS.

- **Hands-On Learning:** The program includes comprehensive assignments, labs, case studies, and a capstone project that simulates a real-world consulting engagement.

- **Open-Source Commitment:** In a groundbreaking move, ICDFA has made the entire CCC curriculum available on GitHub, allowing prospective students and the broader cybersecurity community to explore the materials before applying.

**Applications for the CCC Program will open on January 4th, 2026.** The program is designed for experienced cybersecurity professionals with a minimum of 5 years of experience and a strong understanding of security fundamentals.

The complete curriculum, including study materials, assignments, labs, templates, and resources, is available at https://github.com/icdfa/ccc-program.

**About ICDFA:**

The International Cybersecurity and Digital Forensics Academy (ICDFA) is a leading provider of cybersecurity education and training. Founded by Aminu Idris, AMCPN, ICDFA is committed to developing the next generation of cybersecurity leaders through comprehensive, industry-aligned training programs.

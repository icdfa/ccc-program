# Sample Statement of Work (SOW)

## Cybersecurity Risk Assessment

**Client:** ACME Corporation

**Date:** December 24, 2025

**Project Overview:**

This Statement of Work (SOW) outlines the engagement between [Your Company] and ACME Corporation to conduct a comprehensive cybersecurity risk assessment. The objective of this engagement is to identify, analyze, and evaluate cybersecurity risks to ACME Corporation's critical assets and to provide recommendations for risk mitigation.

**Scope of Work:**

The following activities are in scope for this engagement:

-   **Phase 1: Planning and Scoping:**
    -   Conduct a kick-off meeting to confirm project scope, objectives, and timeline.
    -   Identify and document critical assets and business processes.
    -   Define the risk assessment methodology and criteria.
-   **Phase 2: Risk Identification:**
    -   Conduct interviews with key stakeholders.
    -   Review existing documentation (e.g., policies, procedures, network diagrams).
    -   Conduct a vulnerability assessment of in-scope systems.
-   **Phase 3: Risk Analysis and Evaluation:**
    -   Analyze identified risks to determine likelihood and impact.
    -   Evaluate risks against the defined risk criteria.
    -   Develop a risk register to document all identified risks.
-   **Phase 4: Recommendations and Reporting:**
    -   Develop a set of prioritized recommendations for risk mitigation.
    -   Create a final report detailing the findings and recommendations.
    -   Present the final report to ACME Corporation's management.

**Deliverables:**

-   Risk Assessment Methodology
-   Risk Register
-   Final Risk Assessment Report
-   Presentation of Findings

**Timeline:**

This engagement is expected to be completed within 4 weeks from the project kick-off date.

**Assumptions:**

-   ACME Corporation will provide timely access to all necessary personnel, documentation, and systems.
-   The scope of the engagement is limited to the systems and processes defined in the planning phase.

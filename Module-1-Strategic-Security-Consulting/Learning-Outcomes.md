# Module 1: Learning Outcomes

## Strategic Security Consulting & Business Alignment

Upon successful completion of this module, students will be able to:

### 1. Analyze the Consultant's Role and Responsibilities
- **Competency:** Define the core responsibilities, ethical duties, and value proposition of a cybersecurity consultant.
- **NICE Framework Alignment:**
  - **Work Role:** Security Consultant (SP-RSK-002)
  - **KSA:** K0061 - Knowledge of the organization's enterprise information technology (IT) goals and objectives.

### 2. Evaluate Business Risk and Develop Assessments
- **Competency:** Conduct qualitative and quantitative risk assessments to identify, analyze, and prioritize business risks.
- **NICE Framework Alignment:**
  - **Work Role:** Risk Management Specialist (OV-RSK-002)
  - **KSA:** K0042 - Knowledge of risk management processes (e.g., methods for assessing and mitigating risk).

### 3. Design and Propose Security Strategies
- **Competency:** Develop multi-year security strategies and roadmaps that align with business objectives and risk appetite.
- **NICE Framework Alignment:**
  - **Work Role:** Security Architect (SP-ARC-002)
  - **KSA:** K0058 - Knowledge of the organization's core business/mission processes.

### 4. Apply Foundational Security Frameworks
- **Competency:** Map organizational security requirements to foundational frameworks such as NIST CSF and ISO 27001.
- **NICE Framework Alignment:**
  - **Work Role:** GRC Analyst (OV-PMA-002)
  - **KSA:** K0024 - Knowledge of cybersecurity and privacy principles and organizational requirements (relevant to confidentiality, integrity, availability, authentication, non-repudiation).

# Module 2 Quiz: GRC Deep Dive

## Instructions

This quiz covers the key concepts from Module 2. It consists of 10 multiple-choice questions. Please answer to the best of your ability. The answer key is provided at the end.

---

### Questions

**1. What is the primary purpose of an Information Security Management System (ISMS) as defined by ISO 27001?**
   a) To manage all IT operations in an organization
   b) To provide a systematic approach for managing sensitive company information so that it remains secure
   c) To conduct penetration testing
   d) To respond to security incidents

**2. Under GDPR, what is a "Data Protection Impact Assessment" (DPIA) used for?**
   a) To assess the financial impact of a data breach
   b) To identify and minimize the data protection risks of a project
   c) To report a data breach to the authorities
   d) To request consent from data subjects

**3. Which of the following is a key requirement of the PCI DSS?**
   a) To encrypt all data in the organization
   b) To protect cardholder data
   c) To implement a Zero Trust Architecture
   d) To report all security incidents to the public

**4. What is the primary role of a security auditor?**
   a) To design and implement security controls
   b) To provide an independent and objective assessment of security controls
   c) To manage the security budget
   d) To lead the incident response team

**5. Which of the following is a key principle of ethical consulting?**
   a) Prioritizing the client's interests above all else
   b) Maintaining objectivity and independence
   c) Disclosing confidential information to other clients
   d) Guaranteeing that no security breaches will occur

**6. What is the "right to data portability" under GDPR?**
   a) The right to have your data deleted
   b) The right to move your data from one service provider to another
   c) The right to access your data
   d) The right to object to the processing of your data

**7. Which of the following is a key component of a SOC 2 report?**
   a) A list of all employees and their salaries
   b) An assessment of security, availability, processing integrity, confidentiality, and privacy controls
   c) A detailed financial audit
   d) A penetration testing report

**8. What is the primary purpose of a legal hold in the context of e-discovery?**
   a) To delete all relevant data
   b) To preserve all forms of relevant information when litigation is reasonably anticipated
   c) To encrypt all data
   d) To notify the public of a data breach

**9. Which of the following is NOT a valid reason for excluding a control from an ISO 27001 Statement of Applicability?**
   a) The control is not applicable to the organization's business
   b) The control is too expensive to implement
   c) The risk associated with the control is accepted by management
   d) The control is covered by another existing control

**10. What is the primary focus of the HIPAA Security Rule?**
   a) Protecting the privacy of all personal data
   b) Protecting the security of electronic protected health information (e-PHI)
   c) Protecting the security of financial data
   d) Protecting the security of intellectual property

---

## Answer Key

1. b) To provide a systematic approach for managing sensitive company information so that it remains secure
2. b) To identify and minimize the data protection risks of a project
3. b) To protect cardholder data
4. b) To provide an independent and objective assessment of security controls
5. b) Maintaining objectivity and independence
6. b) The right to move your data from one service provider to another
7. b) An assessment of security, availability, processing integrity, confidentiality, and privacy controls
8. b) To preserve all forms of relevant information when litigation is reasonably anticipated
9. b) The control is too expensive to implement (Cost alone is not a valid reason for exclusion without proper risk assessment and acceptance)
10. b) Protecting the security of electronic protected health information (e-PHI)

## Assignment 2.3: Security Auditing and Assurance - ISO 27001 Internal Audit Report

### Objective

This assignment is designed to assess your understanding of security auditing and assurance and your ability to write a formal ISO 27001 internal audit report. You will be required to write a formal audit report, detailing findings, risks, and recommendations.

### Scenario

**Client:** "DataSafe," a data storage company.

**Background:** DataSafe is a company that provides secure data storage services to a variety of clients. The company is ISO 27001 certified and is required to conduct regular internal audits to maintain its certification. You are an internal auditor for DataSafe and have just completed an internal audit of the company's ISMS.

**Audit Findings:**

During your audit, you discovered the following findings:

-   **Finding 1:** Several employees have not completed their annual security awareness training.
-   **Finding 2:** The company's disaster recovery plan has not been tested in over a year.
-   **Finding 3:** Some servers are missing the latest security patches.
-   **Finding 4:** The company's access control policy is not being consistently enforced.

### Your Task

Based on the scenario and the audit findings, write a formal ISO 27001 internal audit report. The report should be clear, concise, and easy to understand. It should include the following sections:

1.  **Executive Summary:** A high-level summary of the audit findings and recommendations.
2.  **Introduction:** An overview of the audit, including the scope, objectives, and methodology.
3.  **Findings:** A detailed description of each audit finding, including the criteria, condition, cause, and effect.
4.  **Risk Assessment:** A risk assessment for each finding, including the likelihood and impact of the risk.
5.  **Recommendations:** A set of recommendations for addressing each finding. The recommendations should be specific, measurable, achievable, relevant, and time-bound (SMART).
6.  **Conclusion:** A summary of the overall audit results.

### Submission Format

Please submit your audit report as a single Markdown file. The report should be professionally written and formatted.

### Evaluation Criteria

Your audit report will be evaluated based on the following criteria:

-   **Completeness:** Does the report include all of the required sections?
-   **Accuracy:** Is the report accurate and well-justified?
-   **Clarity:** Is the report clear, concise, and easy to understand?
-   **Actionability:** Are the recommendations actionable and realistic?

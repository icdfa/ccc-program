# Module 2: Learning Outcomes

## Governance, Risk, and Compliance (GRC) Deep Dive

Upon successful completion of this module, students will be able to:

### 1. Implement ISO 27001 and Develop a Statement of Applicability (SoA)
- **Competency:** Design and implement an Information Security Management System (ISMS) based on ISO 27001 and create a comprehensive SoA.
- **NICE Framework Alignment:**
  - **Work Role:** GRC Analyst (OV-PMA-002)
  - **KSA:** K0070 - Knowledge of the organization's legal and regulatory requirements.

### 2. Analyze and Apply Advanced Compliance Frameworks
- **Competency:** Conduct gap analyses and develop remediation plans for advanced compliance frameworks such as GDPR, CCPA, HIPAA, and PCI DSS.
- **NICE Framework Alignment:**
  - **Work Role:** Compliance Specialist (OV-LGA-002)
  - **KSA:** K0267 - Knowledge of laws, regulations, policies, and ethics as they relate to cybersecurity and privacy.

### 3. Conduct Security Audits and Provide Assurance
- **Competency:** Plan and execute internal security audits, document findings, and provide assurance on control effectiveness.
- **NICE Framework Alignment:**
  - **Work Role:** Security Auditor (OV-AUD-001)
  - **KSA:** K0321 - Knowledge of audit and assessment methodologies.

### 4. Evaluate and Apply Legal and Ethical Principles
- **Competency:** Analyze complex scenarios involving legal and ethical considerations in cybersecurity consulting and recommend appropriate courses of action.
- **NICE Framework Alignment:**
  - **Work Role:** Legal Advisor (OV-LGA-001)
  - **KSA:** K0048 - Knowledge of laws, regulations, policies, and ethics as they relate to cybersecurity and privacy.

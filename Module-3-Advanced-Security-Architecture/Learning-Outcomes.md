# Module 3: Learning Outcomes

## Advanced Security Architecture & Solution Design

Upon successful completion of this module, students will be able to:

### 1. Design Enterprise Security Architectures
- **Competency:** Develop and document comprehensive enterprise security architectures using frameworks such as TOGAF and SABSA, including the design of Zero Trust Architectures.
- **NICE Framework Alignment:**
  - **Work Role:** Security Architect (SP-ARC-002)
  - **KSA:** K0073 - Knowledge of security architecture concepts and enterprise architecture reference models.

### 2. Develop Secure Cloud Strategies
- **Competency:** Create and implement secure cloud migration strategies, including the selection of appropriate cloud service models and security controls for AWS, Azure, and GCP.
- **NICE Framework Alignment:**
  - **Work Role:** Cloud Security Architect (SP-ARC-003)
  - **KSA:** K0177 - Knowledge of cloud computing service models (e.g., IaaS, PaaS, SaaS) and security implications.

### 3. Manage Vendor and Technology Lifecycles
- **Competency:** Evaluate and manage third-party vendor risk, develop secure procurement processes, and integrate security into the technology lifecycle.
- **NICE Framework Alignment:**
  - **Work Role:** Supply Chain Risk Manager (OV-RSK-003)
  - **KSA:** K0261 - Knowledge of supply chain risk management standards, processes, and practices.

### 4. Design Incident Response and Business Continuity Plans
- **Competency:** Develop and document comprehensive Incident Response Plans (IRPs) and Business Continuity Plans (BCPs) aligned with business requirements and industry best practices.
- **NICE Framework Alignment:**
  - **Work Role:** Business Continuity Planner (OV-PMA-001)
  - **KSA:** K0018 - Knowledge of business continuity and disaster recovery continuity of information and continuity of their impact on security operations.

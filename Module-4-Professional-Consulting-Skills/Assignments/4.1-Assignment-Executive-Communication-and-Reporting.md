## Assignment 4.1: Executive Communication and Reporting - Board Presentation

### Objective

This assignment is designed to assess your ability to communicate complex technical findings to a non-technical audience. You will be required to prepare and deliver a 10-minute presentation to a simulated Board of Directors on the state of security and proposed investments.

### Scenario

**Client:** "Manufacturing Co.," a large manufacturing company.

**Background:** Manufacturing Co. has recently been the victim of a ransomware attack. The attack caused significant disruption to the company's operations and resulted in a large financial loss. The Board of Directors is concerned about the company's security posture and has asked you, a cybersecurity consultant, to present to them on the state of security and to propose a set of security investments.

**Your Findings:**

- The company's security posture is weak.
- The company is not compliant with industry best practices.
- The company's employees have not received adequate security awareness training.

### Your Task

1. **Prepare a 10-minute presentation:** The presentation should be targeted at the Board of Directors and should be clear, concise, and persuasive. The presentation should include the following:
    - An overview of the current state of security.
    - A summary of the key risks and vulnerabilities.
    - A proposed set of security investments, including a cost-benefit analysis.
    - A call to action.
2. **Create a slide deck:** The slide deck should be professional and well-designed. It should use visuals to help communicate your message.

### Submission Format

Please submit your work as a single Markdown file containing:

1. A link to your slide deck (you can use a free online presentation tool like Google Slides or Canva).
2. A script for your 10-minute presentation.

### Evaluation Criteria

Your submission will be evaluated based on the following criteria:

* **Clarity and Conciseness:** Is your presentation clear, concise, and easy to understand?
* **Persuasiveness:** Is your presentation persuasive and compelling?
* **Professionalism:** Is your slide deck professional and well-designed?
* **Actionability:** Is your call to action clear and actionable?

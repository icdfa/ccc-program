## Assignment 4.2: Consulting Project Management - Project Plan

### Objective

This assignment is designed to assess your understanding of consulting project management and your ability to develop a detailed project plan. You will be required to develop a detailed project plan and communication matrix for a 6-month security consulting engagement.

### Scenario

**Client:** "Tech Innovators," a fast-growing technology company.

**Background:** Tech Innovators has engaged your consulting firm to help them improve their security posture. The engagement is expected to last for 6 months and will involve a variety of activities, including a security assessment, a risk assessment, and the development of a security roadmap.

### Your Task

1. **Develop a detailed project plan:** The project plan should be comprehensive and should include the following:
    - The project scope, goals, and objectives.
    - The project timeline, including key milestones and deliverables.
    - The project budget, including a breakdown of costs.
    - The project team, including roles and responsibilities.
    - The project risks and a plan for mitigating them.
2. **Develop a communication matrix:** The communication matrix should identify all of the key stakeholders in the project and should specify how and when you will communicate with them.

### Submission Format

Please submit your work as a single Markdown file containing:

1. Your detailed project plan.
2. Your communication matrix.

### Evaluation Criteria

Your submission will be evaluated based on the following criteria:

* **Completeness:** Does your project plan include all of the required sections?
* **Realism:** Is your project plan realistic and achievable?
* **Clarity:** Is your project plan clear, concise, and easy to understand?
* **Thoroughness:** Is your communication matrix thorough and well-thought-out?

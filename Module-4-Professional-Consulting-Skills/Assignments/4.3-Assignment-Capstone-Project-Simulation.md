## Assignment 4.3: Capstone Project Simulation

### Objective

This assignment is the capstone project for the Certified Cybersecurity Consultant (CCC) program. It is a comprehensive, end-to-end simulated consulting engagement that will require you to apply all of the knowledge and skills that you have learned throughout the program. The capstone project is designed to give you a realistic experience of what it is like to work as a cybersecurity consultant.

### Scenario

**Client:** "Fintech Solutions," a financial technology company that provides a mobile payment platform.

**Background:** Fintech Solutions is a fast-growing company that is facing a number of cybersecurity challenges. The company has recently experienced a data breach that exposed the personal information of thousands of its customers. The company is also struggling to keep up with the ever-changing regulatory landscape. The company has hired you, a cybersecurity consultant, to help them improve their security posture.

### Your Task

You are required to produce a professional consulting report package that includes the following deliverables:

1. **Statement of Work (SOW):** A formal SOW that outlines the scope, objectives, and deliverables of the engagement.
2. **Security Assessment Report:** A detailed report that assesses the company's current security posture and identifies key vulnerabilities and risks.
3. **Security Roadmap:** A multi-year security roadmap that outlines a prioritized set of recommendations for improving the company's security.
4. **Executive Summary Presentation:** A high-level presentation that summarizes the key findings and recommendations for the company's executive leadership.

### Submission Format

Please submit your work as a single zip file containing all of your deliverables. The deliverables should be professionally written and formatted.

### Evaluation Criteria

Your capstone project will be evaluated based on the quality of your deliverables. The deliverables will be assessed on their technical accuracy, strategic relevance, and professional presentation. The capstone project is worth 40% of your final grade for the CCC program.

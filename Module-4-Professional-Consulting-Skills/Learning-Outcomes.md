# Module 4: Learning Outcomes

## Professional Consulting Skills & Capstone Project

Upon successful completion of this module, students will be able to:

### 1. Communicate Effectively with Executive Stakeholders
- **Competency:** Develop and deliver clear, concise, and persuasive presentations and reports for executive-level audiences, translating technical findings into business impact.
- **NICE Framework Alignment:**
  - **Work Role:** Executive Cyber Leadership (OV-EXL-001)
  - **KSA:** K0260 - Knowledge of how to communicate complex technical information to non-technical and executive audiences.

### 2. Manage Consulting Projects and Engagements
- **Competency:** Apply project management principles to plan, execute, and manage cybersecurity consulting engagements, including scope, schedule, budget, and stakeholder management.
- **NICE Framework Alignment:**
  - **Work Role:** Program Manager (OV-PMA-002)
  - **KSA:** K0180 - Knowledge of project management principles and methods.

### 3. Synthesize and Apply Knowledge in a Capstone Project
- **Competency:** Integrate and apply knowledge and skills from all modules to complete a comprehensive, end-to-end simulated consulting engagement, from SOW to final report.
- **NICE Framework Alignment:**
  - **Work Role:** Security Consultant (SP-RSK-002)
  - **Task:** T0529 - Provide advice and input for security enterprise architecture.

### 4. Prepare for Professional Certification Exams
- **Competency:** Demonstrate readiness for advanced cybersecurity certifications such as CISSP, CISM, and CRISC by understanding the core domains and exam objectives.
- **NICE Framework Alignment:**
  - **Work Role:** Cybersecurity Instructional Curriculum Developer (OV-TEA-001)
  - **KSA:** K0062 - Knowledge of industry and government standards for cybersecurity.
